my name is noelle e curry

Noelle Curry is a visual communication design student at San Francisco State University, originally from San Diego. She transferred to the Bay Area last August so is still new to the city and SF state campus. Noelle dearly misses her family and friends she left in San Diego but is really enjoy the new scenery and opportunity to learn at SF state. She also really admires all of her campus's beautiful nature and wildlife like ravens and bumble bees.

When Noelle isn’t working on homework, she loves to be outside in sunshine and nature because she thinks it’s awesome and makes her feel good. Another thing about her is that she loves to swim, especially in the ocean. Noelle thinks that the ocean and sunshine fix all her problems and refreshes her mind.

Being creative in general is very enjoyable for Noelle and she loves the problem solving and imagination it takes. Noelle loves to charcoal draw, watercolor, and cook because of those things. She gets her passion for creativity from her mom who loves to craft things and has a small business hand dyeing yarn and knitting objects.

Noelle also has a big heart for her family. She is the youngest of six kids and now an aunt to two nephews who recently joined the family. One of her nephews is 4 and the other is turning One this September. Noelle had to be older and move away to realize how much she loves and appreciates her family even though they can be annoying.
