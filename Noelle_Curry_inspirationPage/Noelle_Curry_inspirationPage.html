my name is noelle curry

these are a few artists that influence me

kandisky

kandinsky is an inspiration to me as an artist because he highlights things i struggle with as an artist and for me that is finding balance in asymmetry and color. i personally really love balanced asymmetry and i find it very visually pleasing, but it can be hard for me to achieve it is he is a big inspiration to me. i also have a very hard time choosing color palettes, but i adore how many colors Kandinsky brings to a canvas, and i think it is very beautiful.

nyrb

nyrb books also give me a lot of creative inspiration as well, specifically because i really appreciate how simple and understated their books and the covers are. i love their methodology behind the books they publish and how they select a piece of art for the cover that reflects the mood of the book; i think they are great.

led zeppelin

led zeppelin’s music and specifically their album art gives me a lot of design inspiration as well. i know that jimmy page influenced a lot of the art direction for their albums and that many artists and designers worked on their album art. album art is what got me interested into design and i think that Led Zeppelin had such unique are that really reflects their music.
